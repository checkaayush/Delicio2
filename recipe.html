Grilled Vegetable Fajitas

Directions

  1. Heat gas grill to medium-high or prepare charcoal grill with medium-hot coals. In a bowl, stir lime juice, cumin, chili powder, salt, cayenne.
  2. Place onion rings on large plate
  3. Place asparagus in a large resealable plastic bag, peppers in a second bag and squash and zucchini in a third
  4. Coat all vegetables with nonstick spray and grill, covered. Grill asparagus 10 minutes, turning often, and onion, 8 minutes, turning once.
  5. Heat tortillas according to package directions. Spread each with 2 tablespoons hot refried beans, then 3/4 cup mixed grilled vegetables
